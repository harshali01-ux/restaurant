WHO WE ARE.
There's nothing cooking about food. Not our food. Not our people. And definitely not the way we live life.
Around here, we don't settle for anything less than food we're proud to serve.
And we don't just cook in. Not when we can also become our best, make friends, and have fun while we're at it.
We're the Dining Restaurant that lives people life Best by cooking.
We're not for people who want to blend in: pushing boundaries is part of our heritage. We have more than 16,000 restaurants and 350,000 team members in more than 100 countries.
Whether it's the original Stuffed burger or putting a pizza in outer space, we never stop driving ourselves to deliver hot and fresh food, fast every time - anywhere you want to enjoy it.
WHAT WE'RE ABOUT.
At Restaurant, we don't just make food. We make people happy. Rooftop was built on the belief that Dinner night should be special,
and we carry that belief into everything we do. With more than 35 years of experience under our belts,
we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles: We create food we're proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.
WHERE WE COME FROM.
In 1985, two brothers borrowed 6000 from their mom to open a Restaurant place in Pune, PCMC.
They named it ROOFTOP, because their sign only had room for seven letters. How profound! Soon, the restaurant grew. Why? The pizza was awesome. The service felt like home.
And the customers were treated like family. And we've been delivering that same food and service ever since.
